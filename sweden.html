Sweden: Sarek National Park

Trekking in Sarek National Park is a raw and unforgettable adventure through one of Europe's last true wildernesses. Located in Swedish Lapland above the Arctic Circle, Sarek is known for its rugged mountain ranges, deep valleys, wild rivers, and complete lack of marked trails or accommodations, offering a true back-to-nature experience. Hikers must be self-sufficient and prepared for rapidly changing weather, but the rewards are immense—encounters with reindeer herds, panoramic views of untouched landscapes, and a profound sense of solitude and connection with nature. It is a challenging yet deeply rewarding destination for experienced trekkers seeking authenticity and adventure.

For more information visit the Swedish Tourist Association website.
