Norway: Jotunheimen National Park

Jotunheimen, meaning "Home of the Giants," is known for its dramatic and rugged terrain, with many mountains exceeding 2,000 meters in altitude. Trekking in Jotunheimen National Park, Norway, offers an unforgettable experience with breathtaking landscapes, including towering peaks, deep valleys, and crystal-clear lakes, making it a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. The park boasts stunning scenery, including the famous Besseggen Ridge, which offers panoramic views of two lakes separated by a narrow, jagged ridge. The terrain varies from rocky trails and boulder fields to lush valleys and glacial areas, providing a range of challenges and experiences.

For more information visit Jotunheimen National Park website.
